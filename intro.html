Introduction

We, M. Abhinaya and Dhanush Nagarajan, are both Master's in Computer Science students at Georgia State University, and we loved working on this project.

Recipe Studio gave us a chance to apply what we've learned in web programming—semantic HTML, CSS layout and design systems, responsive design, and accessibility—to build something clear and useful. From planning with Kanban and daily standups to styling every page and testing on different devices, the whole process was rewarding.

We hope you enjoy exploring the site. Start with Home or jump straight to our Recipes.
