Tomato Basil Pasta

Serves 4 · Prep 10 min · Cook 20 min

Simple, fresh pasta with ripe tomatoes and basil. No cream—just good olive oil, garlic, and summer flavors. Ready in under 30 minutes.

Ingredients

  • 12 oz spaghetti or linguine
  • 3 tbsp olive oil
  • 3 cloves garlic, minced
  • 2 cups cherry or grape tomatoes, halved
  • ½ tsp red pepper flakes (optional)
  • 1 cup fresh basil leaves, torn
  • Salt and black pepper to taste
  • Parmesan cheese for serving

Instructions

  1. Bring a large pot of salted water to a boil. Add the pasta and cook according to package directions until al dente. Reserve ½ cup pasta water, then drain.
  2. While the pasta cooks, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic and cook until fragrant, about 1 minute. Add the tomatoes and red pepper flakes (if using). Cook, stirring occasionally, until the tomatoes soften and release their juices, about 5–7 minutes.
  3. Add the drained pasta to the skillet. Toss with the tomato mixture, adding reserved pasta water a little at a time if needed to coat the pasta. Remove from heat and stir in the basil. Season with salt and pepper.
  4. Serve immediately with grated Parmesan and extra basil if desired.
