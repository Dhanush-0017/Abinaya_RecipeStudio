Classic Beef Burger

Makes 4 burgers · Prep 10 min · Cook 10 min

Juicy beef patties with simple seasoning, served on toasted buns with your favorite toppings. A backyard and weeknight favorite.

Ingredients

  • 1½ lb ground beef (80/20)
  • 1 tsp salt
  • ½ tsp black pepper
  • 1 tsp garlic powder (optional)
  • 4 burger buns
  • Lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles for serving
  • Ketchup, mustard, or mayo as desired

Instructions

  1. Divide the ground beef into 4 equal portions and gently shape into patties slightly wider than your buns. Make a small indent in the center of each so they stay flat when cooking. Season both sides with salt, pepper, and garlic powder if using.
  2. Heat a grill or skillet over medium-high heat. Cook patties 4–5 minutes per side for medium, or until desired doneness. Add cheese in the last minute if you like.
  3. Toast the buns cut-side down on the grill or in a pan. Serve patties on buns with lettuce, tomato, onion, pickles, and condiments.
